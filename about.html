Our Story

I AM NOMAD is a team that since 2012 has been introducing guests from around the world to the culture, traditions and nature of Kyrgyzstan.

We stood at the origins of major projects dedicated to nomadic heritage, including the World Nomad Games. That experience helped us deeply understand our country’s culture and learn how to tell its story in a way that stays in the memory.

Today we create journeys in which Kyrgyzstan unfolds through living experiences. National games, horse shows, eagle hunting, music, crafts, traditions and genuine Kyrgyz hospitality become part of every itinerary. We organize individual and group tours, cultural programs and special events, helping guests see not only the most beautiful places in Kyrgyzstan, but also feel its true spirit.
