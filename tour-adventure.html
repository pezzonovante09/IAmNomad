Day-by-Day Program

• Airport meeting and hotel transfer. • Rest after the flight. • Bishkek city tour. • Welcome dinner with national cuisine. • Overnight in Bishkek.
• Breakfast. • Drive to Jeti-Oguz gorge. • Hotel check-in. • ATV tour on mountain trails, red rocks and panoramic viewpoints. • Free time for walks and photos. • Dinner. • Overnight in Jeti-Oguz.
• Breakfast. • Horseback ride on scenic mountain routes. • Transfer to the north shore of Issyk-Kul. • Hotel check-in. • Free time on the beach. • Dinner by the lake. • Overnight on the north shore of Issyk-Kul.
• Breakfast. • Wakeboarding with a professional instructor. • SUP boarding and water activities. • Free time on the beach. • Evening cruise on Lake Issyk-Kul. • Dinner on board at sunset. • Overnight on the north shore of Issyk-Kul.
• Breakfast. • Transfer to the rafting site. • Briefing and rafting on a mountain river. • Lunch after rafting. • Return to Bishkek. • Farewell dinner. • Overnight in Bishkek.
• Breakfast. • Free time in Bishkek. • Optional: paragliding; buggy; helicopter tour; SPA and massage; shopping and souvenirs. • Evening in the city. • Overnight in Bishkek.
