Your Kyrgyzstan, Your Way

A journey written for you

Not every traveler wants a ready-made schedule. Some come for silence on a high pasture, others for horse games and eagle hunting, others for mountain trails or a quiet week by Issyk-Kul. A custom tour with I AM NOMAD is a private itinerary built around your interests, pace and dates.

Since 2012 we have been opening Kyrgyzstan to guests from around the world — through living culture, real hospitality and landscapes that stay in the memory. We do not sell a template. We craft a route that feels personal from the first day to the last.

From a yurt night at Song-Kul to freeride lines and helicopter views over the Tian Shan, we assemble experiences that fit you — guided by a team that knows the country beyond guidebooks.
