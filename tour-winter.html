White Peaks of Kyrgyzstan

Luxury Winter Expedition

Day-by-Day Program

• Airport meeting. • Transfer to a premium hotel. • Rest after the flight. • Welcome dinner. • Tour program briefing. • Overnight in Bishkek.
• Breakfast. • Transfer to Karakol. • Hotel check-in. • Skiing or snowboarding. • Après-Ski. • SPA and sauna. • Dinner. • Overnight in Karakol.
• Full day of skiing. • Optional private lessons with an instructor. • Skiing on groomed slopes. • Evening at a bathhouse complex. • Dinner. • Overnight in Karakol.
• Breakfast. • Outing with professional mountain guides. • Freeride on untouched snow. • Picnic in the mountains. • Return to the hotel. • SPA. • Dinner. • Overnight in Karakol.
• Breakfast. • Transfer to the new all-season mountain cluster Ala-Too Resort. • Skiing on modern lifts and slopes. • Panoramic viewpoints. • Après-Ski. • Dinner. • Overnight at the resort. (The first phase of the resort opens gradually in 2026; infrastructure is actively developing.)
• Breakfast. • Helicopter tour over the Tian Shan range. • Panoramic views of glaciers. • In favorable weather — scenic flight toward the Khan Tengri area (no landing; route depends on weather and permits). • Return. • Farewell dinner. • Overnight in Bishkek.
• Breakfast. • Free time. • Airport transfer.
