Классический Кыргызстан

7 дней природы, культуры и кочевого наследия

Day-by-Day Program

• Airport meeting and hotel transfer. • Rest after the flight. • Bishkek city tour. • Walk around Ala-Too Square. • Welcome dinner with national cuisine. • Overnight in Bishkek.
• Breakfast. • Visit to the Burana Tower historical complex. • Walk through the Stone Garden. • Scenic drive to Song-Kul Lake. • Check-in at a traditional yurt camp. • National dinner. • Evening by the high-altitude lake. • Overnight in the yurt camp.
• Breakfast. • Master class on assembling a Kyrgyz yurt. • Demonstration of traditional horse games. • National lunch. • Free time for walks, rest and photos. • Optional horseback ride along the lake. • Dinner. • Overnight in the yurt camp.
• Breakfast. • Drive through scenic mountain passes. • Eagle hunting demonstration in Bokonbaevo. • Check-in at a hotel on the south shore of Issyk-Kul. • Dinner by the lake. • Overnight at Issyk-Kul.
• Breakfast. • Transfer to Karakol. • Off-road transfer to Altyn-Arashan gorge. • Check-in at a guesthouse. • Walk through one of Kyrgyzstan’s most beautiful gorges. • Dinner. • Overnight in Altyn-Arashan.
• Breakfast. • Horseback ride through the gorge. • Relax in natural hot springs. • Lunch. • Off-road descent to Karakol. • Return to Bishkek. • Overnight in Bishkek.
• Breakfast. • Free time for walks and souvenir shopping. • Visit to Osh Bazaar or TSUM. • Farewell dinner at a national restaurant. • Airport transfer.
